Extra Information

Welectricity is gaming educational app based on different power sources. The target user is 6-12 years-old child. The final score will generated by how much power of power sources generated, how much to build power sources and pollution score that power sources impact to the area where you placed. Hope you have fun this game! Enjoy the game! :)
